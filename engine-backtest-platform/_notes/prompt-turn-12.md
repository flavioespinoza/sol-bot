# Turn 12 — Zoom, Pan, and Crosshair

Add interactivity to the candlestick chart:

- **Zoom:** mouse wheel to zoom in/out on the X axis
- **Pan:** click and drag to scroll through time
- **Crosshair:** on hover, show a vertical line at the cursor and display the OHLC values + date for that bar in the chart header

The equity curve chart should stay synced — when you zoom or pan the candlestick chart, the equity curve follows.
